WHAT IS A CANVAS TENT?

Canvas is a woven material usually made from cotton. Army duck cotton canvas is a thicker weave of cotton that lends additional sturdiness to the canvas fabric, especially when it’s double-fill cotton canvas. The best canvas tents are built from 100% army duck cotton canvas. Canvas makes for an excellent fabric of choice for a heavy duty canvas tent because of how durable and breathable the material is. Canvas is also generally a more sustainable option for your canvas camping tent and offers natural water resistance when the fibers swell to close the pores to keep water out. The most popular types of canvas tents are: Canvas wall tents Canvas bell tents Canvas cabin tents
